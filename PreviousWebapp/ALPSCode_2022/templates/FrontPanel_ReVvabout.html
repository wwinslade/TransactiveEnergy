About ReVv

Energy management is a major focus of modern technological research. The Smart Home Energy Management System (SHEMS) can lower peak energy usage by informing users of their energy consumption and further encouraging them to be more mindful of their energy usage habits during peak load hours. By providing interactive visualizations about the user's energy cosumption and by giving users a host of tools for greater control over their energy usage, the Smart Home Energy Management System can have a major impact on how we use our energy.
