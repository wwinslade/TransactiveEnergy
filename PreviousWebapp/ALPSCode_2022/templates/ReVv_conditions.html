About ReVv

According to EIA forecasts, the electricity consumption in the United States will increase by 2.1% in 2021 after falling 3.8% in 2020. Electricity sales to the industrial sector are expected to grow by 4.2% in 2021 while electricity sales to the residential sector will grow by only 2.3% in 2021. This increase is primarily a result of colder temperatures in the first quarter of 2021 compared with the same period in 2020. Much of the increased electricity consumption across the sectors reflects improving economic conditions in 2021. For 2022, the forecast expects U.S. electricity consumption will grow by another 1.3%. It is expected that energy consumption will continue this trend upward with growing prominence of electric vehicles.
